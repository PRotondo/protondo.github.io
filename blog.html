Coin tossing

Theorem
Let \(T\) be the number of bits it takes to decide to which interval \(I_j\) the number \(X\) belongs. Then we have $$ H(Y) \leq \mathbb{E}[T] \leq H(Y) + 2\,, $$ where \(H(Y) = \sum_{j=1}^k p_j \,\log_2(1/p_j)\) is called the entropy of \(Y\).
Furthermore, the \(+2\) in the bound is tight by our example with the uniform distribution.
